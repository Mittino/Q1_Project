At Adopt-A-Dog we feel like every pooch deserves a good home. This is why we’ve created a website to help you search for dogs in your local area. If you find a dog you’re interested in, you can submit an application on our website. We require that the shelter ID of the dog is included so that we can connect you with the shelter or rescue group who is working with the animal. We wish you all the best with the search for your forever pet!
